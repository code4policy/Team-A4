Three sections, to fully walk you through what's going on

We offer to take you through three main sections, each dealing with a key aspect of the new space race:

  1. The Private Sector Gold Rush: With the dramatic reduction in the costs of launching to space, the private sector has made massive technological and business inroads in bringing to market the innovations that space makes available to mankind. How is it going? You'll find the answers in there!
  2. The Path of Space Exploration: Since Sputnik, mankind has made tremendous progress in populating Earth's orbits with a wide array of satellites, doing a number of things for us every day. Every nation is taking it at its own pace, with very different outcomes. You will be able to get a detailed picture here.
  3. The Future of the Space Industry: Many say that the space industry has reached a plateau, that innovation cannot go on forever, and that we will soon be hitting diminishing returns as to what space can deliver concretely for us on Earth. Is this true? You will be walked through this in this section!
